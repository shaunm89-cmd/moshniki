How to Install

1

Prepare your Device

On your Kogan Dongle, go to Settings > System > About. Click "Android TV OS Build" 7 times to unlock Developer Mode.

2

Get Downloader

Go to the Google Play Store on your TV, search for "Downloader" and install it.

3

Install Unlinked

Open Downloader and enter the code for Unlinked. Grant permissions when asked.

4

Download Our Apps

Open Unlinked and enter our Library Code. Download Ausmate and AusVod directly to your device.
