üë∂ Jellybean Baby

When a baby Koala (Joey) is born, it's only 2cm long! That's the size of a jellybean. It has no hair, no eyes, and no ears.

üß† Smooth Brain

Koalas have very smooth brains. They aren't very smart! If you put leaves on a plate, they don't know it's food. It MUST be on a branch!

üëç Two Thumbs

Look at your hand. You have one thumb. Koalas have TWO thumbs on each front paw to help them grip trees tightly.

üíß No Water?

The word "Koala" comes from an Aboriginal word meaning "no drink". They get almost all their water from eating juicy leaves.
