The Giraffe

The tallest mammals on Earth and the coolest neck-stretchers in the savannah. Did you know a giraffe's neck is too short to reach the ground? They have to spread their legs just to drink water!

Height: Up to 5.5 meters (18 ft)
Tongue Color: Purple/Black (Natural sunscreen!)
Sleep: Only 30 minutes a day!
